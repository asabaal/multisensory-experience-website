Risk-Sharing Model

Revenue Share Collaboration

This contract mode is designed for builders who want to contribute to new and experimental projects. It's a risk-sharing arrangement where payment is tied to success and revenue generation from the project or product.

Why This Model?

Revenue share aligns incentives and attracts true builders who want to build something meaningful together. Both parties share in the upside, creating genuine partnership rather than transactional relationship.

When This Works Best

  • Building something new and experimental
  • Outcomes are uncertain but upside potential is high
  • Risk should be shared between parties
  • Cash flow is limited or constrained
  • You want ownership in what you help create

How It Works

  • Contribution to a defined project with clear goals
  • Revenue share tied to that specific project or product
  • Clear scope and deliverables agreed upfront
  • Exit conditions defined at the start
  • Payment aligned with success and revenue generation
  • No upfront payment required from either party
