Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about investing with Asabaal Ventures.

What makes Asabaal Ventures unique as an investment opportunity?

What are the minimum investment requirements?

Our current equity investment minimum is $50,000 for the Digital Foundation phase. Revenue-sharing notes begin at $25,000. For physical Community Center partnerships, investments start at $250,000. We also offer custom investment packages for strategic partners and institutional investors.

What is your expected timeline to profitability?

Our digital platform is projected to reach operational profitability by Q4 2025. Individual community centers are expected to achieve profitability within 12-18 months of opening. Our phased approach allows us to establish revenue streams and validate our model before scaling to physical locations.

What are the primary risks associated with this investment?

As with any early-stage venture, risks include market adoption rates, execution challenges, and the evolving regulatory landscape. Specific to our model, we face risks related to scaling our digital-to-physical strategy and the capital requirements of community center development. Our detailed risk assessment and mitigation strategies are available in our full investor prospectus.

Do you accept international investors?

Yes, we welcome qualified international investors subject to applicable securities regulations and compliance requirements. Our investment structures can accommodate various jurisdictions, though specific terms may vary. Please contact our investment team for details related to your location.
