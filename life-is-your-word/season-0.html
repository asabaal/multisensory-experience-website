Daughter

When religious leaders called a bleeding woman "unclean" for twelve years, Jesus broke every rule to meet her. This episode explores the difference between human labels and God's calling.

Biblical Reading

Mark 5:25-34 - The story of the woman who suffered from bleeding for twelve years and was healed by touching Jesus' cloak. Jesus called her "daughter" instead of "unclean."

The host shares their experience as a queer person rejected by those who claim Christ's name. Despite trauma that made scripture and worship music painful, Jesus restored their relationship with Him and His Wordâ€”not through religious condemnation, but through authentic faith.

Key Insights

  • Religion can steal from your relationship with God, but faith restores it

  • Jesus meets us in our brokenness, not our perfection
